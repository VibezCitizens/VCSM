# Vibez Citizens — User Journey Guide

How the app works from creating an account to running your business.

---

## 1. Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Sign Up

When you open Vibez Citizens for the first time, you'll see the **"Join Vibez Citizens"** screen.

- Enter your **email address**
- Create a **password** (must be 8+ characters with uppercase, lowercase, and a number)
- Confirm your password
- Tap **"Create account"**

Green checkmarks appear next to each password rule as you meet them. The button stays disabled until everything is valid.

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile

After signing up, you'll see **"Complete Your Profile"** where you fill in:

- **Your name** — this is what people will see on your profile
- **Username** — the system generates one for you (like @jorsh4821). This becomes your handle.
- **Birthdate** — pick your date of birth
- **Sex** — Male or Female

Tap **"Save & Continue"** and you're in. You now have a Citizen account.

---

## 2. Updating Your Name and Profile Picture

### Where to go

Open **Settings** → **Profile** tab.

### What you'll see

- Your current avatar (profile picture)
- Your banner image (background header)
- Your display name
- Your username (locked — you can't change it)
- Your email (locked)
- Your bio

### Changing your name

- Tap the **Display name** field
- Type your new name
- Tap **"Save"** at the bottom

### Changing your profile picture

- Tap **"Choose image"** under your avatar
- Select a photo from your device (PNG or JPG, max 5MB)
- The new photo appears immediately as a preview
- Tap **"Save"** to confirm

### Changing your banner

Same process — tap **"Choose banner"**, select an image, save.

A green **"Saved"** message appears briefly to confirm your changes went through.

---

## 3. Creating a Vport (Your Business Page)

A **Vport** is your business identity on Vibez Citizens. You can create multiple Vports — one for each business you run.

### Where to go

Settings → Vports → **"Create new Vport"**

### What you fill in

**Profile tab:**
- **Business name** (required) — e.g., "Tony's Gas Station"
- **Type** (required) — pick from 80+ business types across categories like:
  - Gas & Fuel → gas station
  - Food & Hospitality → restaurant, baker, chef, caterer
  - Beauty & Wellness → barber, hairstylist, nail technician
  - Professional & Business → exchange, consultant, lawyer
  - And many more
- **Avatar** (optional) — your business logo or photo
- **Description** (optional) — tell people what your Vport is about

**Services tab:**
- Shows service options specific to your Vport type
- Toggle on the services you offer
- These will be visible on your public Vport profile

Tap **"Create Vport"** and your business page is live.

---

## 4. Switching Between Citizen and Vport

Once you create a Vport, you'll see a **floating switcher widget** in the bottom-right corner of the app. It shows your available identities.

### How to switch

- Tap the identity you want to switch to (e.g., "vport" or "user")
- The app instantly switches your active identity
- Everything you do now happens as that identity

### What changes when you switch

- Your name and avatar change to the selected identity
- Posts you create will be from that identity
- Messages will show you as that identity
- Dashboard access changes based on identity type

### Switching back

Tap the switcher again and select your Citizen (user) identity. You're back to your personal account.

## 5. Vport Dashboards — Managing Your Business

Each Vport type gets a custom dashboard with tools specific to your business. Access it at your Vport's dashboard page.

---

### Gas Station Vport

#### Updating Gas Prices

From your dashboard, tap **"Fuel Pricing"**.

**Official Prices:**
- You see your current prices for each fuel type (Regular, Premium, Diesel, etc.)
- Tap a price to edit it
- Enter the new price
- Save — the change is live immediately

**Community Suggestions:**
- Customers can submit price suggestions when they visit your station
- You'll see pending suggestions in your dashboard
- For each suggestion, you can:
  - **Approve** — the suggested price becomes your official price automatically
  - **Reject** — optionally add a reason why

This keeps your prices accurate even when you're not at the station.

---

### Exchange Vport (Money Exchange)

#### Updating Exchange Rates

From your dashboard, tap **"Exchange Rates"**.

**Setting rates:**
- Select the **base currency** (e.g., USD)
- Select the **quote currency** (e.g., MXN)
- Enter your **buy rate** (what you pay)
- Enter your **sell rate** (what you charge)
- Tap **"Save"**

**Managing rates:**
- View all your active currency pairs
- See buy/sell spreads at a glance
- Edit or delete any pair
- Timestamps show when each rate was last updated

Customers visiting your Vport profile will see your current rates.

---

### Restaurant Vport

#### Creating Your Menu

From your dashboard, tap **"Online Menu"**.

**Adding categories:**
- Create categories like "Appetizers", "Main Courses", "Drinks", "Desserts"
- Reorder categories by dragging them

**Adding menu items:**
- Select a category
- Tap **"Add Item"**
- Fill in:
  - **Item name** (e.g., "Tacos al Pastor")
  - **Description** (e.g., "Marinated pork with pineapple, cilantro, and onion")
  - **Price** (e.g., $8.99)
  - **Photo** (optional — snap a picture of the dish)
- Save the item

**Other menu tools:**
- **QR Code** — generates a scannable code that links to your online menu. Print it and put it on your tables.
- **Flyer** — create a printable PDF menu you can hand out or post on your wall.
- **Show/hide items** — toggle items on and off without deleting them (great for seasonal items)

---

### Barber / Beauty Vport

#### Managing Appointments

From your dashboard, tap **"Calendar"** or **"Bookings"**.

**Setting your availability:**
- Set your **operating hours** (open and close times)
- Toggle which **days** you're available (Sunday through Saturday)
- **Block specific dates** for holidays or days off
- Set **appointment duration** (30 minutes, 60 minutes, etc.)

**Viewing appointments:**
- See your calendar in day, week, or month view
- Pending bookings appear highlighted
- Confirmed bookings show the customer's name and service requested
- Cancelled bookings are shown separately

Customers can book available time slots directly from your Vport profile.

---

### All Vport Types — Common Features

Every Vport, regardless of type, has access to:

- **Services** — toggle which services you offer from a catalog specific to your business type
- **Reviews** — see what customers say about you, sorted by rating or date
- **Settings** — edit your Vport name, avatar, banner, description, hours, and contact info
- **Public Profile** — customers see your services, ratings, and business details when they visit your page
