**Fix 2 — BW-001: cancelBooking — terminal status guard (prevents mutation replay)**

**Fix 1B — ELEK-003: createBooking — pin customerActorId to requestActorId on citizen path**
- File: `engines/booking/src/controller/createBooking.controller.js`

  - After validation: `customerActorId = requestActorId` (always pins on citizen path)
- Prevents caller from supplying a victim's actorId as `customerActorId` to create an IDOR chain
