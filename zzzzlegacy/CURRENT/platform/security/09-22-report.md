## 4. Marketplace Interactions

### Business Creation & Ownership

- Any authenticated user can create a VPORT (business listing)
- 60+ vport_types supported (artist, barber, chef, mechanic, restaurant, gas_station, etc.)
- Ownership linked via `vc.vports.owner_user_id` and `vc.actor_owners`
- No verification requirement — no `is_verified` field exists
- No limit on VPORTs per user
- Tables: `vc.vports`, `vc.vport_public_details`, `vc.actors` (kind='vport')

### Services & Pricing

| Feature | Table | Transparency |
|---|---|---|
| Service catalog | `vc.vport_services` | Public (key, label, category) |
| Menu categories | `vc.vport_actor_menu_categories` | Public when active |
| Menu items + pricing | `vc.vport_actor_menu_items` (`price_cents`, `currency_code`) | Public when active |
| Fuel prices | `vc.vport_fuel_prices` | Public (`price`, `currency_code`, `unit`) |
| Fuel price submissions | `vc.vport_fuel_price_submissions` | Internal (review workflow) |
| Exchange rates | `vc.vport_rates` (`buy_rate`, `sell_rate`) | Public |
| Price tier indicator | `vc.vport_public_details.price_tier` (1-4) | Public |

### Booking System

- Customers book services from VPORTs
- Table: `vc.bookings` with status lifecycle: pending → confirmed → completed / cancelled / no_show
- Customer PII collected: `customer_name`, `customer_phone`, `customer_email`, `customer_note`
- Source tracking: public / owner / admin / import / sync
- Overlap prevention via `booking_range` (tstzrange generated column)
- Tables: `vc.booking_resources`, `vc.booking_availability_rules`, `vc.booking_availability_exceptions`

### Reviews

- Any authenticated user can review any active VPORT
- No transaction/booking verification required
- `is_verified` defaults to `true` (misleading)
- One review per author per VPORT (UNIQUE constraint)
- Reviews can be soft-deleted by author
- Dimension ratings per vport_type (230 dimension configs)
- Tables: `vc.vport_reviews`, `vc.vport_review_ratings`, `vc.vport_review_dimensions`

**ToS implications:**
- Business owners represent they are authorized to act for the business
- Business owners are responsible for accuracy of listings, pricing, contact info
- Platform is not a party to transactions — facilitates connections only
- Reviews must be honest; fake reviews prohibited
- Platform reserves right to remove misleading listings or reviews
- Booking disputes are between service provider and customer

## 6. Messaging and Communications

### Chat System

| Component | Table | Detail |
|---|---|---|
| Conversations | `chat.conversations` | DM and group, with visibility/access modes |
| Messages | `chat.messages` | Text, image, video, file, audio, system, event types |
| Members | `chat.conversation_members` | Role (member/admin), permissions (can_post, can_manage, can_moderate) |
| Inbox | `chat.inbox_entries` | Per-user inbox with folder (inbox/spam/archived), read/delivery tracking |
| Receipts | `chat.message_receipts` | Delivered/read status per message per actor |
| Typing indicators | `chat.typing_states` | Transient presence with `expires_at` |

### Message Properties

- **Encryption:** Optional client-side encryption (`body_ciphertext`, `body_nonce`, `body_alg` = xchacha20poly1305)
- **Key management:** `chat.conversation_keys` (wrapped keys per conversation)
- **Retention:** Indefinite — no auto-purge mechanism
- **Edit:** `edited_at` timestamp tracked
- **Delete (user):** Soft hide via receipts (local only)
- **Delete (admin):** Hard delete of message row, logged in `chat.audit_log`
- **Idempotency:** `client_id` UUID prevents duplicate sends
- **Sequence:** `conversation_seq` ensures ordered delivery

### Wanders (Async Postcards)

- Customizable card templates (birthday, valentine, business, photo, generic)
- Can be sent to email, SMS, or link
- Anonymous sender support (`is_anonymous` flag)
- Recipient tracking: `recipient_actor_id`, `recipient_email`, `recipient_phone`
- Reply threads on received cards
- Card lifecycle: draft → sent → opened → expired → deleted
- TTL support: `expires_at` timestamp

### Notification System

- Database-driven via `vc.notifications`
- Real-time via Supabase Realtime (PostgreSQL change streams)
- Types: follow, follow_request, follow_request_accepted, comment, like, dislike, rose, mention, booking, review
- Read tracking: `is_read`, `is_seen` flags
- No per-type muting or preferences yet
- Subscriptions: `badgeSubscriptions.js` listens to `chat.inbox_entries` and `vc.notifications` changes

**ToS implications:**
- Messages are private between conversation participants
- Platform may access messages for moderation/legal compliance
- Platform may delete messages that violate community standards
- Notifications are system-generated, not opt-out configurable
- Wanders may be sent to non-users via email/SMS

## 11. Legal Risk Areas

### HIGH RISK — Must be addressed in ToS

| Risk | Description | Recommendation for ToS |
|---|---|---|
| **Incomplete account deletion** | vc schema data (posts, messages, bookings, reviews) persists after deletion | Disclose that some content may be retained; define what "deletion" means |
| **No consent tracking** | No mechanism to record ToS/Privacy Policy acceptance | Legal consent system now implemented (09-21 task), must be activated with seed data |
| **No business verification** | Anyone can create fake business listings | Include clause that impersonation/fake listings are prohibited; platform not liable for listing accuracy |
| **Unverified reviews** | Reviews auto-verified without transaction proof | State reviews are user-generated opinions; platform does not verify reviewer-business relationship |
| **No moderation queue UI** | Reports filed but can't be actioned by staff | Disclose best-effort moderation; no guarantee of response time |
| **No suspension/ban enforcement** | Schema supports it, no implementation | Reserve the right to suspend/ban; implement before launch |
| **Customer PII in bookings** | Phone, email, name stored without retention policy | Disclose data collection in booking context; define retention period |
| **Location data** | Lat/lng collected and stored, default public for pins | Disclose location data usage; clarify voluntary nature |

### MEDIUM RISK — Should be addressed in ToS

| Risk | Description | Recommendation |
|---|---|---|
| Platform as marketplace facilitator | Bookings/services between users | Clear intermediary disclaimer — not party to transactions |
| User-generated content license | Platform needs rights to display content | Include content license grant in ToS |
| Child safety | No age verification beyond self-reported birthdate | State minimum age requirement (13+); no responsibility for misrepresentation |
| Cross-border data | US + Latin America markets | Note governing law jurisdiction; data may be processed in multiple countries |
| Message encryption | Optional, not enforced | Don't promise E2E encryption; note messages may be accessed for moderation |
| Media permanence | CDN URLs are public and may be cached | Warn that shared media may persist even after deletion |
| Guest/anonymous users | Wanders allows anonymous interaction | Define limited rights for anonymous/guest users |
| Learning system (LMS) | Embedded in VCSM, separate from Wentrex | May need separate educational data terms (FERPA implications) |

### LOW RISK — Consider for completeness

| Risk | Description |
|---|---|
| Notification opt-out | No per-type notification preferences |
| Reaction semantics | Rose/gift system has no monetary value currently |
| Design documents | Private creative tools — IP ownership unclear |
| Fuel price crowdsourcing | Submission workflow — accuracy disclaimer needed |

## Summary: What the Terms of Service Must Reflect

### What users can do:
- Create accounts, profiles, and business listings
- Post content (text, images, video), comment, react
- Send messages, create conversations
- Book services, write reviews
- Send virtual postcards (wanders)
- Follow, block, report other users
- Share location data
- Upload media
- Access embedded learning features

### What the platform allows:
- Public social interactions and content sharing
- Marketplace connections between service providers and customers
- Business listing creation and management
- Booking facilitation
- Encrypted private messaging
- Anonymous postcard sending
- Real-time notifications

### What the platform can enforce:
- Content removal (hide or delete)
- User warnings
- Account suspension (time-limited or permanent)
- Account banning
- Conversation muting and member removal
- Access revocation
- Actor voiding (identity disabling)
- Report investigation and resolution

### What responsibilities users and businesses have:
- Accurate profile and listing information
- Compliance with community standards
- Authorization to represent businesses
- Accurate pricing and service descriptions
- Honest reviews
- No fake listings, spam, harassment, or illegal activity

### What liabilities the platform limits:
- Not a party to user-to-user transactions
- No warranty on service quality from VPORTs
- Best-effort moderation, not guaranteed
- No liability for user-generated content accuracy
- Platform provided "as is"

### What rights the platform reserves:
- Content moderation and removal
- Account suspension and termination
- Data retention for legal/safety purposes
- Content license for platform operation
- Terms modification with notice
- Cooperation with law enforcement
