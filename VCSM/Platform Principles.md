Vibez Citizens
Platform Principles

These principles guide technical and product decisions.

One Identity Graph

Everything in Vibez Citizens is represented as an Actor.

Actors include:

• Citizens
• Vports

This unified identity system allows the same actions across the platform.

Actors can:

• follow
• post
• message
• review
• interact

The Actor system is the foundation of the entire platform.

Social Layer

Content and interactions create a social graph.

Key elements:

• Vibes (posts)
• Sparks (comments)
• Follows
• Reviews
• Reactions

This layer drives discovery and engagement.

Place Layer

Vports represent real-world places and services.

Vports can publish structured information:

• menus
• services
• fuel prices
• exchange rates
• amenities
• contact details

This creates a living database of local services.

Communication Layer

The Vox system enables communication between actors.

Actors can:

• message each other
• start conversations
• manage threads

Messaging connects discovery with real-world action.

Trust Layer

Trust is created through:

• reviews
• moderation
• reporting
• reputation

The trust system protects the community and ensures reliable information.

3. Vibez Citizens
Roadmap Vision (5-Year Direction)

This roadmap describes where the platform is heading, not exact timelines.

Phase 1 – Core Network

Goal: Build the foundation.

Features:

• Citizen accounts
• Vport profiles
• Vibes (posts)
• Sparks (comments)
• Vox messaging
• reviews
• follows
• Citizen Central feed

The objective is to create a living network of users and businesses.

Phase 2 – Local Discovery

Goal: Become the best place to discover local services.

Features:

• improved search
• location-based discovery
• structured Vport data
• reviews and ratings
• service catalogs

Vibez Citizens becomes a social discovery platform for real-world places.

Phase 3 – Community Layer

Goal: Support communities.

Feature:

Districts

Districts allow users to form communities around:

• neighborhoods
• cities
• interests
• events

Districts become the community infrastructure of the platform.

Phase 4 – Commerce Layer

Goal: Enable economic activity.

Possible features:

• booking services
• ordering products
• service requests
• tipping
• transactions

At this stage Vibez Citizens becomes a local digital marketplace.

Phase 5 – City Network

Goal: Become a platform for real-world infrastructure.

Examples:

• live fuel prices
• exchange rates
• local events
• community updates
• service availability

The platform becomes a digital reflection of city life.
