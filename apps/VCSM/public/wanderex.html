What it is

WanderEx is the public-facing layer of Vibez Citizens. When you create a VPORT, you get a clean, mobile-ready profile page at a permanent URL — no setup required.

Anyone who visits that link sees your full profile: who you are, what you offer, when you're available, and how to book — all without needing an account.
